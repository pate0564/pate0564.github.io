Subcategory: Graphics Design

Tools Used

Technical Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

Description

Moodboard was designed as parallel research for creating resume. The most important reason to design moodboard is that it illustrates my personal brand image. It includes a color palette, two to three font options for headers, paragraphs, illustrations, photography and creative vector graphics. The most importantly moodboard is a style guide or also called as visual guide for any brand.
